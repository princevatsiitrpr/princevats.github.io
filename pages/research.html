Research Experience

Projects & Focus Areas

Exploring the intersection of atmospheric science, environmental engineering, and artificial intelligence to tackle real-world air quality challenges.

Ongoing Project

Environmental Quality Monitoring System Using Low-Cost Sensors for Urban Air Quality Monitoring

  • Developing monitors using multiple low-cost sensors for monitoring PM1, PM2.5, PM10, CO2, temperature, pressure, humidity, noise levels, CO, wind speed, and TVOCs.
  • Deploying a dense network across urban areas with real-time data transmission to a custom-made central server.
  • Implementing CNN-Bidirectional LSTM model for anomaly detection, spatio-temporal analysis, and forecasting for 24h, 7d, and 1-month horizons.
  • Supporting policy-making through real-time urban air quality insights.
